Project Description


The Library Management System (LMS) is a digital solution designed to automate and simplify the management of a library's core functions. It helps librarians and users manage books, track issued and returned items, and maintain student or member records with ease.
The system provides a centralized platform where book availability can be checked, due dates are monitored, and fines can be calculated automatically. It reduces the need for manual paperwork and improves the overall efficiency of library operations.
Developed with user-friendly interfaces, the LMS ensures that both administrators and students can interact with the system easily. It is ideal for educational institutions such as schools, colleges, or training centers seeking a smart and modern approach to library management.
